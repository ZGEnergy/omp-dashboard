Lazy-loaded on first text-file open (≈1 MB gzip chunk). The 6 MB TypeScript language worker is dropped — v1 is read-only, syntax highlight via the main-thread Monarch tokenizer, no LSP. Per-kind viewers: Monaco (code), ImageViewer (/api/file/raw + pan/zoom), PdfViewer (<object>), MarkdownViewer (canonical renderer), BinaryWarn (open-externally notice). Coexists with code-server (full IDE) and native editors — nothing removed.
