Classic — every pinned folder stays fully expanded; the chevron manually collapses one at a time (today's behavior).
Accordion — only the folder of the selected session shows full detail. Other folders compact to their header and surface just the sessions that need attention (ask_user, streaming, active, or unread). Click any header to focus that folder.

When off, unfocused folders collapse to header-only with a N sessions — click to view row (no card peeking). When on, sessions demanding attention still show through.
